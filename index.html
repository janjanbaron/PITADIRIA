About Us

Pita Dira was founded with the mission to showcase the diverse variance of authentic Filipino cuisine infused with various flavors with a twist using pita bread to create a unique dining experience. Our passion for combining traditional flavors with a modern twist has led us to create a variety of delicious fillings such as adobo and caldereta. Our team is dedicated to providing fresh, high-quality ingredients and exceptional service.
